For this tekken guide I will be using Hwoarang as a way to provide an example of tekken 8's combo system. Hwoarang has some of the highest damage in the game. Below is a video example of this.



As you can see in the above video, tekken 8 has a lot of new system mechanics to play with. You will notice that I can extend the combo using heat burst, similar to an instal but universal to the whole cast.
In this state (which is limited and tied to a bar under the health bar) you have access to a mechanic called heat dash and heat smash. This is what I used to get the opposing player to the wall. After this happens you will
further notice that the wall, which is glowing red, is caused to explode, leading to further combo damage. This is a far cry from the systems present in tekken 7.



As you can see, the difference in what is possible is quite polarizing. When learning Tekken 8 it is important to take time to learn the new systems as best you can. While Tekken 8 boasts easier base
mechanics such as moving, it brings a lot of new depth to the table for anyone looking to really sink their teeth in.
